About BetaBuilders

A startup ecosystem for learning by doing.

BetaBuilders is a community-driven startup ecosystem connecting contributors with early-stage digital products. Founders, volunteers, developers, designers, testers, marketers, HR contributors, content creators, and product thinkers collaborate remotely on meaningful projects.

This is a volunteer contribution and internship-style learning environment, not a traditional employment program. Contributors build skills, strengthen portfolios, and gain exposure to startup workflows through honest, practical contribution.

Certificate Section

Certificate and reference letters are contribution-first.

Certificates and reference letters are not automatic. They may be considered only after meaningful and verified contribution, professional communication, completed work, and project approval.

  • ✓Verified contribution and meaningful participation
  • ✓Completed assigned tasks and quality work
  • ✓Professional communication and reliable records
  • ✓Project lead approval after review
  • !No certificates for only joining, creating a profile, attending one meeting, false claims, backdated requests, incomplete tasks, or unverified work.

University Documentation

Check your institution's requirements before applying.

Some applicants may need documents for university, college, work-integrated learning, course placement, internship requirements, migration, or other academic purposes.

BetaBuilders and its startup projects do not provide offer letters, placement agreements, insurance documents, supervisor declarations, attendance guarantees, fixed hour guarantees, pre-filled college forms, or documents confirming work not yet completed.

Documentation requests, if considered, must be based on actual completed contribution and reviewed by authorised leadership. Approval is not guaranteed.

FAQ

Questions applicants ask most.

Are all roles remote?+

Yes. All roles are remote.

Are all roles unpaid?+

Yes. All roles are volunteer and unpaid.

Is this paid employment?+

No. These are volunteer contribution and internship-style learning opportunities, not paid employment.

Can beginners apply?+

Yes. Beginners can apply if they are willing to learn, communicate clearly.

Do I get a certificate automatically?+

No. Certificates or reference letters are not automatic. They depend on verified contribution and project approval.

Do you provide college or university documents?+

Not automatically. Documentation is not guaranteed and must be reviewed by authorised leadership.

Can you confirm hours before I start?+

No. Hours can only be confirmed based on verified contribution.

Can you backdate my joining date?+

No. Backdated dates and false claims are not accepted.

Does platform access guarantee a certificate?+

No. Platform access does not guarantee certificates, references, documents, or hours.

Do you create PDF or Word role documents?+

No. This website is the official information hub.

Can this count toward my college internship?+

It depends on your college or university rules. Applicants must check with their institution before applying.

Can I choose my startup project?+

You can express interest, but placement depends on project needs, skills, and availability.

Are roles flexible?+

Yes. Opportunities are flexible, but task availability and timelines depend on project needs.

What information should I include when applying?+

Send a LinkedIn message with your role interest, project interest, current skills, portfolio links, availability, timezone, and a short introduction.

What records are not accepted?+

False joining dates, fake hours, inaccurate work history, backdated contribution records, and misleading role claims are not accepted.
